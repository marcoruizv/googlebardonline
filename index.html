Google BARD AI

Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers for Adversarial Robustness

Introduction to Google BARD AI

Google BARD AI is an advanced natural language processing (NLP) model that focuses on improving adversarial robustness in language understanding tasks. It extends the capabilities of BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) by incorporating techniques to enhance the model's resistance to adversarial attacks.
